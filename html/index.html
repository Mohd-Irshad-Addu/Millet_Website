Who We Are ?

Millet-e-Islamia High School was established in the year 2000. Our school provides co-education from UKG to 10th grade, with Urdu as the medium of instruction. Despite this, we also focus on English and Telugu to ensure that students excel in all fields. In addition to academic education, we impart Islamic teachings to our students, helping them succeed in both this world and the hereafter. Computer education is also an integral part of our curriculum. Many students who have studied at our school have achieved success and are currently serving in various fields.

Our Vision :

  • Our vision is to support students in achieving their academic and personal goals, striving for excellence in all aspects of their lives.
  • In addition to worldly education, we also provide Islamic teachings to ensure that our students excel both in this world and in the hereafter.
  • Our school ensures equal opportunities for all students, regardless of background, to help them reach their full potential.
  • We provide a creative and innovative learning environment where students are encouraged to explore new ideas and develop critical thinking skills.
  • We offer a range of extracurricular activities, sports, and arts programs to ensure the overall development of our students.
