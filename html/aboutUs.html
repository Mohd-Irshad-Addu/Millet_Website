About Us

School Overview

Millet-E-Islamia High School, established in 2000, offers co-education from UKG to 10th grade with Urdu as the medium of instruction, while also focusing on English and Telugu. Along with acadmic Education, we provide Islamic teachings to guide students in both this world and the hereafter. Computer educatin is also an essential part of our curriculum.
We prioritize individual attention for each students and offer extra classess to help weaker students imrpove, Additionally, we encourage physical activity by conducting ground actvities three times a week(Thursday, Friday, and Saturday) to help students stay active and develop a well-rounded personality.

Sports and Activities

Our school offers various sports activities and encourages students to participate in them. Every week, from Thursday to Saturday, we take the students to the ground for sports sessions. Apart from sports, we also focus on other activities like public speaking, drawing contests, science fairs, and social activities such as cleanliness drives. These activities help students grow in different areas and teach them the importance of teamwork, creativity, and responsibility.

Islamic Education Program

Every year, Bhatkal Education organizes an exam for children in our school, where the students are taught the basics of Islam, such as duas, the correct method of performing namaz, and other essential teachings, After the lessons, an exam is conducted and the top two students receive silver medals, while the remaining students are given gifts. This exam is only for students from the 6th to the 10th grade, and there is no fees to participate.

Technology & Computer Lab

Our school has a Computer Education Lab where students learn important computer skills. The lab is well-equipped with modern computers and software, providing hands-on experience in courses like MS-Office , Typing and basic computer use. This lab helps students stay up-to-date with technology and prepares them for future academic and career success.
